Scope

ShapeOS is an end-to-end smart-home platform that fuses real-time IoT control with an AI-driven predictive energy optimization engine. Built on Flutter and Firebase with an ESP32 edge controller, the system continuously monitors household appliances, learns usage patterns over a 14-day window and commits enforceable peak-hour auto-off rules that the engine applies automatically. A conversational AI agent powered by Google Gemini handles voice control, anomaly response and room-level automation, while a profile-band safety detector guards against voltage surges, current leakage, motion, smoke, flame and gas events. The result is a single intelligent operating layer for the modern home that lowers bills, prevents hazards and runs itself.

Key Features
Predictive Energy Optimization
14-day usage analysis surfaces peak-hour waste and commits enforceable auto-off rules.
Conversational AI Agent
Gemini 2.5 tool-calling brain controls devices, sets thresholds and remembers facts.
Room-Wise Control & Bills
Group devices by room, split monthly bills and track per-room consumption history.
Wake-Word Voice Mode
Hands-free continuous conversation — natural commands, TTS replies, exit phrases.
Anomaly Auto-Cut
Profile-band detector triggers cut-offs on voltage surges and current leakage.
Tariff-Aware Billing
Off-peak 55 PKR / kWh, peak 70 PKR / kWh — live daily and monthly bill estimates.
Full Action Audit
Every manual, automated and agent-driven change logged with reason and source.
Live Safety Sensors
Motion, smoke, flame and gas alerts with FCM push and on-screen banners.
